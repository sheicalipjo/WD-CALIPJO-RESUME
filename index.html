ARTISTS IN NEED PH CHARITY (2018-2021)

Self-Employed / Under Independent Contracts

A charity founded in May 5, 2019, it aimed to be a non-profit for artists who want to start their digital art journey but are not able to buy the starter equipment. FREELANCE LIVE2D RIGGER / VTUBER MODEL ARTIST (2020-Present)

Self-Employed / Under Independent Contracts

Going along with the 2020 VTuber trend, Sheillice took high interest in the functions of Live2D, working for various independent VTubers for months developing their VTuber models and 2.5D animations.

ARTIST ALLEY EXHIBITOR / MERCHANT (2024-Present)

Various Art Markets (Patrons of the Arts, Komiket, Sticky Expo, etc.)

A new business venture that Sheillice has been eyeing ever since 2015, she finally had the chance to try out selling her art in an art market. It is a positive change and while, like most businesses, revenue will not come back quick, she believes it is a good work experience as it puts her in the spot of endorsing and advertising while also have the opportunity to network.
